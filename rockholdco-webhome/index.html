Aktuala Loko

Where are you, right now? And can you show me how to get to where you are? Sometimes the simplest thing is to just show your friends a map, with your current location at the exact dead center. With Aktuala Loko, you can just tweet your current location. Now your followers can really follow you.

Regex Live!

With Regex Live!, learning how to use regular expressions is easy. Type in a few sentences typical of the text you're searching through, and experiment with your regex until you get the effect you want. See exactly the part of the text that your regex matches; even see the matched substrings, color-coded for clarity. Become a regex expert!

Available for the iPhone, iPod Touch, and iPad since November 2011.
