Regex Live!

Current version: 1.0
Platforms: iPhone, iPod Touch, and iPad
OS version: 5.0 or newer
Price: Free!

With Regex Live!, learning how to use regular expressions is easy. Type in a few sentences typical of the text you're searching through, and experiment with your regex until you get the effect you want. See exactly the part of the text that your regex matches; even see the matched substrings, color-coded for clarity. Become a regex expert!

Features

  • Instant feedback; a green indicator shows immediately whether your regular expression is valid

  • Add as many example text strings as you like, so see what your regex really matches

  • The matching part of the example strings is indicated in bold text

  • Any matching substrings are indicated with colored text, so you can develop an intuitive grasp of how regexes work

  • Blank characters in your example strings are rendered visible for greater conceptual clarity
